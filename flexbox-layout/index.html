The way we work

To provide maximum flexibility and transperancy for our clients, we use Agile frameworks to build custom solutions.

The work is planned and delivered in two-week iterations. At the end of each iteration, completed work is demonstrated to the client, and the client has the opportunity to repriotize and tweak requirements for the next iteration.

Discover

We start with a conversation. Our goal is to understand your buisness needs at a high level and determine if blockchain technology can offer the right solution. With short iterations, priorities can be adjusted in real-time to ensure we deliver the highest value.

Plan & Design

Here's where start getting into the weeds. We spend time with your team to understanding your technical requirements and take them away to devise a plan. We return with a proposal and estimated costs.

Proof of concept

Our team will spend two weeks delivering a proof-of-concept that demonstrates blockchain technology applied to your particular buisness use case. See the potential for yourself and adjust your expectations.
